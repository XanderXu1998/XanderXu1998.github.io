Secrets about Study

Posted on 2019-04-10

Some facts I found during my student career:

  • There is no royal road to learning.

    Maybe you can save time by find currect method, but the work you need to do is . There is no way to learn something need 3 years with 3 days; you can just prepare 3 days for a semester-term course’s exam, because the exam just need 3 days to pass. You can never learn what a person has learned with a semester efforts, he built solid structure and long-term memory, while you just built fragile structure which will necessarily slide just after a few weeks.

    Remember, you can’t short the time you need to be a master. What you need to cost must be cost in your way, early or later. For example, if you want to be a computer scientist, you need to know a lot of knowledge of math and computer, if you haven’t study those in your college, you need to study in your work.

  • The most powerful tip is practice on your learning zone.

  • Focus and modest are the most important qualities for a learner.

  • Note and practice are important for long-term memory.

    Maybe you can go through your school life without any notes taken. But you will finally realize that faced with such a brilliant era you really need notes to deal with immense information. The way to a expert is full of structure, you need notes to help you form long-term memory.

  • Skill and Structure are the aim of study.

    Most studies are aim to a skill. But also a lot of studies are aim to build a structure to get a deeper understand of something. If you want to learn programming or playing piano, just know the knowledge is far from need, you need practice. If you go through a lot of courses with a lot of chips, they will disappear after a short period.

  • Deliberate practice is very important.
