Michigan Test

The test organized by Michigan University consists of five kinds of exams. After comparing the exam format in the officail web with that in the announcement, I make sure that the exam we will take is MTELP. After that, I take a practice from a website.

I get 47/50 in the reading section but get only 29/50 in the listening section. After having a glance at the format of speaking and writing section, I go to the examination room with the perturbed mood. The examiner is an Asian.

We take reading section firstly, which is a little different from what I practice before: it consists of grammer, vocabulary and reading sections. Then we take the writing section because there is a problem with the stereo speaker. It’s a disaster. I nearly failed in finishing the essay. The next section is listening, a disaster too. Finally comes the speaking section, in which the examiner asks me two questions: who influenced you most in your youth, and what is the most difficult thing you ever experienced. I answer with Einstein and the college entrance examination. Last he told me that even though I felt sad after the college entrance examination, the college I entered is a very good college. Maybe it just depends on your mind that how about your college.

The email of the examiner is tranthu@msu.edu.

In addition, I met a cute girl who took the exam with me today. But I haven’t ask for her name because of shyness.

Dinner with Ruping

Posted on 2019-03-26

Today My good friend Ruping come back from England, and we take dinner together.

We talk a lot, from dreams to quantum computing, from the life in English campus to the plan for the future life. There is a lot of fun.

Ruping tells me that how she argued with her professors about the feedback of a paper course, and found in surprise that the feedback of the whole department became the format that Ruping had asked.

I think it’s amazing. She changed the feedback format of the whole department, now and future! When the students in future ask professors why the format is like this, professors will answer that it has been this since a Chinese student asked. WOW! How can you know the result before you have a try?

What’s more, I think the very big difference between the college in England and ones in China is humanization. They will respect you when you have done well in any area. They care about your personality very much, what’s nearly ignored by Chinese universities. There are so many students and professors have so little time, as a result most of them don’t care about who you are but just care about the rules.

Moreover I think she has a big and valuable view. She knows how English, French, German and Italian feel like. She knows they are just the same as us, however I can hardly realize this.

In addition, we talk about the cost of living in England. The yearly tuition is 27000£, gas fee 150£ per month, rent 90£-100£ per week, and food 50£ per month. It’s really expensive.

Anyway, it’s a fun day.

I should send an email today and study English and QC hard. But I saw a beautiful Ph.D. in my area, then I watched her Weibo for the whole day. Oh my God!!!
