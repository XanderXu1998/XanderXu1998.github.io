Caesar's Life

Posted on 2019-03-24

Until today, I am about 20.775 years old, and now is nearly the 8:18 in my life.

When I first used the app named “生辰”, it’s just 7:59. 1 minutes of the clock are equal to 2 weeks in the life. And 20 minutes passes by, which means 40 weeks are gone.

I just have lived for about 7000 days from my born. What I understand is, most people of this era live for a long but boring life, just repeat one day thousands of times. Or we can say, for nearly all people, most of whose lives are meaningless, just living for living. Only the very limited period of one’s life matters. Only the very limited period makes something great valuable.

You should be careful of the meaningless thing fulled in your daily life.

I getup in 2:00 p.m. today. I used to have a practice contest of TOEFL afternoon, but I waste the time.

At night I eat, have my hair cut and practice the piano, cost about 2 hours. Then I begin to write the diary and download books for my kindle.
